Block tags are elements that take up the entire width of their container whileinline tags take up as much width as their content needs.

Margin is the space outside the border of an element, creating space between the element and other elements. Border is the edge of an element, which wraps around the padding and content and Padding is the space inside the border, between the content of an element and its border.

The css units we can use are divided into relative lengths and absolute lengths. Absolute are cm, mm, in, px, etc and relative lengths are em, rem, etc

You would use a figure tag to put an image in a frame and have texts both above the image and below it. It also comes with figcation to help you with more description.
