Football

Football Hero

Football is a game that is developed in the middle ages, a rough form of football emerged in England, where villages would compete in mass, chaotic games with goals miles apart. These games, known as "mob football", were violent and had very few rules. By the 19th Century, it became popular in schools but had different rules and varied from one place to another. In 1863, a football federation was formed in England to standardize the game and created a set of first official rules for modern football.
