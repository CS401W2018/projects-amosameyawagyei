The African Lion

The African Lion is part of the cat family. It is a large cat of the genus Panthera. They are very much native to Africa and India. The Adult male lions are bigger and stronger in physique than the female Lions. Lions live together in territories. They are able to live for 15-16 years for females and 10 years for male.

Lions are the best in the Animal world when it comes to hunting. They have several hunting skills like ambushing, stalking, and coorperative hunting when they hunt together in pack.
